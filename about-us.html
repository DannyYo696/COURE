WHY COURE?

We are a leading platform services provider established to enable businesses achieve their objectives through the integration of innovative digital solutions that provide insightful information for decision-making. Technology is our forte and we have a track record of leveraging it to support businesses across the world. Incorporated in the United States of America in 1998 and locally in 2008, we are at the forefront of providing accurate, secure, timely and relevant data to our varied clientele base. With over two decades of experience in building platforms, we have distinguished ourselves as a clear leader in this space with solutions such as our All Number Query, Hub and Device Management System platforms.


OUR PEOPLE

COURE provides platforms and solutions for businesses. We focus on the proper implementation of technology to improve Client's business process and operations by automating repeatable functions and activities. This automation helps Clients save time and money and also allows for growth without a significant increase in resources used to manage the growth. Our team of certified professionals focus on creating solutions that is efficient in performance, effective in functionality and most importantly simple to use. Our automated solutions cover various industries and sectors to include solutions for businesses, governments and non-profit organizations.

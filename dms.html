DMS Features

Real-time Transaction Monitoring

Gain a real-time overview of your financial transactions. Track every sale, refund, and payment with precision. Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies instantly.


Technical Status Dashboard

Stay informed about the health of your payment terminals and networks. Our intuitive dashboard provides a clear picture of technical performance, ensuring uninterrupted operations.


Comprehensive Reporting

Generate insightful reports that provide a deep understanding of your business. Analyse transaction trends, financial performance, and operational efficiency effortlessly.


User Access Control

Maintain data security with role-based user access. Grant specific permissions to different users, ensuring that sensitive information is only accessible to authorized individuals.


Financial Transaction Reconciliation

Simplify the reconciliation process. Our platform automates the matching of financial records, streamlining the process and reducing errors.
